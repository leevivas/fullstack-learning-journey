Multimedia (Audio & Video)

Multimedia makes your website more engaging. You can add audio and video directly into your webpages using HTML.

What You Will Learn

  • How to add audio to a webpage
  • How to add video to a webpage
  • How to use controls and attributes

Adding Audio

<audio controls>
  <source src="audio.mp3" type="audio/mpeg">
  Your browser does not support audio.
</audio>

The <audio> tag allows you to embed sound in your page.

Adding Video

<video width="320" height="240" controls>
  <source src="video.mp4" type="video/mp4">
  Your browser does not support video.
</video>

The <video> tag allows you to embed videos.

Common Attributes

  • controls shows play/pause buttons
  • autoplay plays automatically
  • loop repeats the media
  • muted starts without sound

Best Practice

  • Use appropriate file formats (MP3, MP4)
  • Avoid autoplay for better user experience
  • Provide fallback text for unsupported browsers

Try It Yourself

  • Add an audio file to your page
  • Add a video with controls
  • Experiment with autoplay and loop
